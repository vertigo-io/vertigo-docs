# Atoms

Atoms are the elemental building blocks in a design system that, through successive assembly, allow the creation of a web application.

The OpenSource ecosystem is full of UI component libraries. Many exist at an excellent level of quality and support.

Rather than yet again reinventing the wheel, we chose to select one that matched both the general style we wanted to adhere to, whose development team shared convictions in common with Vertigo, and most importantly offered a first-rate level of quality and support.
Another element that might seem trivial to some yet is nevertheless essential: through this choice, we wanted to highlight a European solution.

This choice is also motivated by our conviction that the OpenSource world derives its strength from collaboration and exchange.

For all these reasons, we chose to use the __Quasar__ component library for the atoms of our Design System.
For more details on the various components, you can visit the [documentation](https://quasar.dev/), which is very well done.

The various molecules and organisms of our Design System are composed of atoms from this library.
